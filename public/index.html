A community effort led by your neighbors

Exploring natural gas for Rick Road.

Over the past several months, a group of Rick Road residents has been working with PECO to explore the possibility of bringing natural gas service to our neighborhood.

PECO is currently evaluating whether natural gas service can be extended to Rick Road.

The next step

What is happening right now?

As the next step in the evaluation process, PECO is gathering non-binding applications from homeowners within the proposed project area.

According to PECO, every completed application helps reduce the project’s cost and helps them evaluate the opportunity for Rick Road. So the more of us who add our names, the better it is for the whole street.

Adding your name does not mean:

  • Committing to natural gas service
  • Signing a contract
  • Agreeing to install anything
  • Spending any money

You are simply expressing interest. That’s it.

If you’re unsure

Not sure you would ever switch?

That’s completely okay. Even if you’re unsure whether you would ever connect to natural gas, your name still helps — it helps PECO evaluate the project and helps reduce project costs for everyone on Rick Road.

It takes about five minutes, costs nothing, and doesn’t commit you to anything.
